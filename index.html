About Me

Hey there! I'm Mark Hattori. I'm currently a Senior at Santa Clara University, and I'm majoring in Web Design & Engineering and minoring in Communications. In my free time, I love creating music, going to concerts, watching sports, and having fun with my friends and family.

I started getting into music at a really young age. My mom says that as a toddler, I would watch the drummer play at church and then bang away at the drums once the service was done. Since then, I've picked up piano, guitar and bass as well. In 7th grade, I was exposed to GarageBand for the first time and that experience opened me up to the world of music production. After that, I began to produce my own covers and write my own songs. I also currently play keyboard and guitar in my church's worship band.
